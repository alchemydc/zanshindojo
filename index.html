About Zanshin

Zanshin is a word used commonly throughout Japanese martial arts to refer to a state of relaxed alertness. Literally translated, zanshin means “the mind with no remainder.” Zanshin is being constantly aware of your body, mind, and surroundings without stressing yourself. It is an effortless vigilance.


The Zanshin Dojo is a collective that brings the principles of mindfulness, awareness and vigilance from the traditional Japanese martial arts to the modern world of cryptocurrency operations.


We are a small team of technical professionals who believe that cryptocurrencies can change the status quo, and free people from what is essentially economic bondage. Our team has been involved with BTC since 2011 and presently operates independent infrastructure in support of the Zcash, Ethereum, Decred, Celo and Horizen networks.

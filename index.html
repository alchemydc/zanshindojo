Agoric

Agoric is a Proof-of-Stake chain utilizing secure JavaScript smart contracts to rapidly build and deploy DeFi. Currently in Phase 0 Mainnet, you can stake Agoric BLD tokens now, while rewards will be activated as the next step of the network establishment process.

STAKING USING KEPLR WALLET

1. Install the Keplr wallet

We recommend that you "Import Ledger" and store your BLD securely on your hardware wallet.

2. Stake your Agoric BLD Tokens

Earn rewards on your staked tokens by delegating them to the ZanshinDojo validator.

3. Set amount to delegate

Delegation will earn rewards on your BLD tokens, once rewards are enabled by the community via the governance process. Delegation also exposes you to risk of loss of funds via slashing, which is why you should choose your validator(s) thoughtfully.

Celo

Celo is a mobile-first platform that makes financial dApps and crypto payments accessible to anyone with a mobile phone

Normal users should consider using the Celo Wallet. Note that the web version of the Celo wallet does not support staking, so you'll need to install the desktop version appropriate for your operating system.

This staking guide from Figment provides instructions for how to stake with the Celo desktop wallet

STAKING CELO USING CELOCLI

celocli is a command line tool only recommended for use by advanced users

1. Lock your Celo tokens

Celo tokens must be locked before they can be used to vote. 

celocli lockedgold:lock --from <YOUR_ADDRESS> --value <AMOUNT_TO_LOCK>

2. Vote for the Zanshin Dojo group

Voting will earn you 6% on your locked tokens.

celocli election:vote --for 0x8eb004dad9397b8f23e1279905c584920000756d --from <YOUR_ADDRESS> --value <NUM_VOTES>

3. After 24 hours, activate your votes

Technically, you can activate your vote at the start of the next epoch. See TheCelo to see when the new epoch will begin 

celocli election:activate --from <YOUR_ADDRESS>

4. Done!

About Zanshin

Zanshin is a word used commonly throughout Japanese martial arts to refer to a state of relaxed alertness. Literally translated, zanshin means “the mind with no remainder.” Zanshin is being constantly aware of your body, mind, and surroundings without stressing yourself. It is an effortless vigilance.


The Zanshin Dojo is a collective that brings the principles of mindfulness, awareness and vigilance from the traditional Japanese martial arts to the modern world of Web3 operations.


We are a small team of technical professionals who believe that Web3 can change the status quo and put more power in the hands of the users and creators. Our team has been involved with BTC since 2011 and presently operates independent infrastructure in support of the Agoric, Celo, Decred, Ethereum2, Horizen, Ren and Zcash networks.
